About

My experience Before Tech has mostly been in the restaurant business. I have worked in a variety of different restaurant sizes all the way from large corporate restaurants to small cafes. I worked as a shift leader, server, host, and busser throughout my high school career into my first year of college. I never really liked working in a restaurant, but it did drastically improve my communication skills as well as my ability to work in a team.

After high school, I decided to attend Ball State University to major in computer science. I was very excited to learn how to code and become a developer. After a year and a half, I decided to minor in computer science instead and leave Ball State. Making the dean’s list for two semesters and not feeling like I had learned a single thing was a scary feeling. As I started researching other alternatives, I came across Eleven Fifty Academy.

I am currently attending the Web Development course at Eleven Fifty Academy. So far, it has been one of the best decisions I have ever made in my life. I learned more in the first week at Eleven Fifty than I learned during my entire time at Ball State. I can’t express how excited I am to continue learning so many new valuable skills during the next nine weeks.
